# MeridianAI

> **Enterprise AI Platform for Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI Agents, Enterprise Search, and Intelligent Workflow Automation.**

---

## Overview

MeridianAI is an enterprise-grade AI platform designed to transform organizational knowledge into trustworthy, explainable, and actionable intelligence.

The platform combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Enterprise Search, AI Agents, Long-Term Memory, and Workflow Automation to enable organizations to securely interact with their internal knowledge while maintaining enterprise-grade reliability, security, and scalability.

MeridianAI is being developed following modern software engineering practices with a strong emphasis on modular architecture, documentation, testing, observability, and production readiness.

---

## Vision

To empower every organization to unlock the full value of its knowledge through trustworthy, scalable, and intelligent AI systems.
